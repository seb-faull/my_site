About Me

I am a BA Digital Media Design (Bournemouth University) Graduate.
Having recently graduated with a 2:1 class honours I am looking for a career in User Experience (UX) and software development for iOS (Swift). Throughout my time at University I have advanced my skills in both design and development. My degree has equipped me with a deep theoretical understanding of design and UX as well as the skills to design and build a product from start to finish.

Personal data protection & security are of great interest to me and have a great theoretical understanding about it. Away from the computer I enjoy playing sport. I have an energetic and extroverted personality that just thrives off other people's energy.
